# User Stories - Slot Machine Game

## Overview
This document contains user stories derived from our personas and research. Each story represents a core user need that informs the design and functionality of our slot machine game.

---

## User Story 1: Playing Slots to "Beat" the Casino

**As a** player who wants to feel smart and in control
**I want** to adjust how a slot machine behaves (e.g., higher win frequency modes)
**So that** I can match my risk and reward preference

### Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] How-To/Tutorial to let people know how the machine works
- [ ] Displaying the probabilities and odds of winning
- [ ] Let users select their rewards

### Notes
-[I went down the 'Advantage Player' slot rabbit hole so you don't have to. Here's what I discovered: : r/gambling](https://www.reddit.com/r/gambling/comments/19dqir8/i_went_down_the_advantage_player_slot_rabbit_hole/)
- Slot Machines are false advertising to lure people to play more
- People get played by the machine, and therefore by the Casino
- Targets people who are addicted to slot machines
- We should try to get people to believe that they are winning more than they actually are (within legal limits)

## User Story 2: Error Handling

**As a** player  
**I want** clear messages when something goes wrong
**So that** I understand what to do next and the game doesn't feel broken

### Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] User is notified if balance is too low (not silent)
- [ ] Invalid actions are prevented
- [ ] Messages are clear and visible
- [ ] Option to contact support / FAQ
- [ ] Game state doesn't break after an error

### Notes
- https://www.dreamjackpot.com/blog/slot-malfunctions-explained
- Clicking the button multiple times (Disable while spinning)
- Balance is 0
- Error msgs shouldn't interrupt gameflow

## User Story 3: Thrill Seeker

**As a** thill seeking player
**I want** to win more money without it feeling too easy
**So that** I can play for longer and feel better about my wins

### Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Ability to adjust reward/chance of higher reward
- [ ] Bonus stage as another form of rewarding
- [ ] More tense moments (e.g., fake outs for losses but actually winning)

### Notes (optional)
- Russian Roulette style gameplay where you can set higher risk for higher rewards rather than an increased chance of rewards
- https://community.gamcare.org.uk/forum/overcoming-problem-gambling/for-people-with-slot-machine-addiction/
- First win → wanting more wins, creating a cycle
- Wanting to feel secretive and unseen
- From software perspective, we would WANT people to play more
